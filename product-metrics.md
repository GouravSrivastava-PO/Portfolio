# Product Metrics

Quantified outcomes by company and role. Each metric is anchored to the mechanism that drove it — not just the result.

---

## Basware · Technical Product Owner
*Enterprise SaaS Financial Reporting & Consolidation Platform*

### Delivery Outcomes

- **Team velocity improved ~15%** by introducing SAFe sprint cadence, enforcing a Definition of Ready (DoR) gate before sprint commitment, and eliminating the most common source of mid-sprint scope change: stories entering the sprint without agreed acceptance criteria

- **Sprint completion sustained above 80%** consistently by shifting the backlog hygiene conversation upstream — stories were sized, dependency-free, and stakeholder-signed-off before they were eligible to be pulled into a sprint

- **Rework reduced** by embedding acceptance criteria review into the story refinement session itself, so dev and QA were aligned on edge cases before a line of code was written — not after

- **Onboarding time reduced** by building a structured knowledge base covering the financial consolidation module's data model, workflow logic, and configuration options, replacing ad-hoc knowledge transfer that had been slowing new team members down

## Connoisseur Infotech · Product Owner
*AI Resume Parser + ERP Integrations*

### Delivery Outcomes

- **5 ERP integrations delivered** by defining end-to-end integration specs with clear data contracts, error handling requirements, and acceptance criteria for each integration point — enabling engineering to build without constant clarification loops

- **Operational efficiency improved ~20%** by automating the manual resume-processing workflow through an AI-powered candidate screening pipeline that extracted, scored, and ranked candidates without recruiter intervention at the intake stage

- **Sprint completion scaled from near-zero to 80%+** in under one quarter by introducing structured sprint planning rituals, a backlog grooming cadence, and a Definition of Done that the team had a shared understanding of for the first time

- **Post-release defects reduced** by embedding QA checkpoint requirements directly into user stories — negative scenarios and edge cases were acceptance criteria, not afterthoughts tested in UAT

## Comprinno Technology · Associate Project Manager
*Incident Management & Project Delivery Automation*

### Delivery Outcomes

- **Client feedback turnaround reduced by ~80%** by building a Jira–Slack webhook automation that pushed real-time ticket status updates directly to client-facing Slack channels, eliminating the email chain that had been the primary communication channel

- **Project visibility improved** by configuring role-based Jira dashboards and automating daily standup digests to Slack — giving both internal teams and client stakeholders a live view of delivery progress without requiring a status call

- **Delivery predictability improved** by introducing WIP limits and visual board governance on Kanban, surfacing task bottlenecks before they became sprint-level blockers and reducing idle time between handoff stages
