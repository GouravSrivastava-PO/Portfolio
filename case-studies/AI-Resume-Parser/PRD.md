\# AI Resume Parser - Product Requirements Document



\## Product Overview



AI Resume Parser is a solution designed to automate resume screening and candidate profiling for recruiters and hiring managers.



\## Problem Statement



Recruiters spend significant time manually reviewing resumes, extracting candidate details, and matching profiles against job requirements.



This results in:



\* Slow screening process

\* High manual effort

\* Inconsistent evaluation

\* Delayed hiring decisions



\## Target Users



\### Primary Users



\* Recruiters

\* Talent Acquisition Teams



\### Secondary Users



\* Hiring Managers

\* HR Operations



\## Product Goal



Reduce manual resume screening effort and improve candidate shortlisting accuracy using AI.



\## Key Features



\### Resume Upload



Users can upload resumes in PDF and DOCX formats.



\### Information Extraction



Automatically extract:



\* Name

\* Contact Information

\* Skills

\* Experience

\* Education



\### Candidate Scoring



Match candidate profiles against job descriptions.



\### Candidate Summary



Generate AI-powered candidate summaries.



\## Success Metrics



\* Reduced screening time

\* Improved recruiter productivity

\* Increased shortlisting accuracy

\* Faster hiring decisions



\## Out of Scope



\* Interview scheduling

\* Offer management

\* Payroll processing
